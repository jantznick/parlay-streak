Getting Started

New to Parlay Streak? Here's how to get started:

  1. Create an account by signing up with your email
  2. Browse today's available games and bets
  3. Make your selections and build parlays
  4. Watch your streak grow as your picks resolve

New to the game? Check out our comprehensive How to Play guide for detailed explanations of all game mechanics, bet types, insurance system, and strategy tips.

How Streaks Work

Your streak increases when all bets in a parlay hit. If any bet in a parlay misses, your streak resets to zero. Single bets add +1, 2-bet parlays add +2, 3-bet parlays add +4, 4-bet parlays add +8, and 5-bet parlays add +16.

Insurance System

4-bet and 5-bet parlays are eligible for insurance. Purchasing insurance protects your streak if the parlay misses - you'll only lose the insurance cost instead of resetting to zero. Insurance costs vary based on your current streak level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit a parlay after creating it?

Yes, you can edit parlays until the first game in the parlay starts. After that, the parlay is locked.

How are leaderboards updated?

Leaderboards update in real-time as parlays resolve. Your position changes immediately when your streak updates.

What happens if a game is postponed?

If a game is postponed, bets associated with that game will be voided and won't count toward your streak.
