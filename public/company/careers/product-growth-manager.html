Product & Growth Manager

Remote • Full-time

About the Role

Help drive product strategy and user growth for Parlay Streak. You'll work closely with engineering and design to prioritize features, analyze user behavior, develop growth strategies, and ensure we're building the right things for our users.

This role is perfect for someone who loves the intersection of data, user experience, and product strategy. You'll have a direct impact on how we grow and improve the platform.

What You'll Do

  • Develop and execute product roadmaps aligned with business goals
  • Analyze user data to identify growth opportunities and pain points
  • Conduct user research and gather feedback to inform product decisions
  • Work with engineering to define features and requirements
  • Design and run experiments to test growth hypotheses
  • Track key metrics and report on product performance
  • Collaborate with marketing on user acquisition strategies

Requirements

  • 3+ years of product management or growth experience
  • Strong analytical skills and experience with data analysis tools
  • Experience with user research and understanding user needs
  • Ability to translate business goals into product requirements
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • Experience with A/B testing and experimentation
  • Strong project management and organizational abilities

Nice to Have

  • Experience with gaming or sports platforms
  • Background in competitive or social products
  • Understanding of retention and engagement metrics
  • Experience with analytics tools (Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Technical background or ability to work closely with engineers

Company Culture

At Parlay Streak, we're building a platform that combines sports, competition, and technology. We value:

  • Collaboration: We work closely together, sharing ideas and supporting each other's growth
  • Ownership: You'll have real impact on the product and the ability to shape its direction
  • Learning: We're always exploring new technologies and approaches to solve problems
  • Balance: We believe in sustainable work practices and supporting team members' well-being
  • Sports Passion: Many of us are sports fans, and that enthusiasm drives what we build

You'll have the opportunity to wear multiple hats, learn new skills, and see your work directly impact users. We're remote-first and value clear communication, autonomy, and results.

How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief note about why you'd be a good fit to careers@parlaystreak.com with "Product & Growth Manager" in the subject line.

We review all applications and will reach out if there's a potential fit.
