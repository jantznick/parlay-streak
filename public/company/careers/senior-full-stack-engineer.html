Senior Full-Stack Engineer

Remote • Full-time

About the Role

We're looking for an experienced full-stack engineer to help build and scale our platform. You'll work on both frontend and backend systems, implementing new features, improving performance, and helping shape the technical direction of the product.

You'll be working with modern technologies including TypeScript, React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, building features that directly impact user experience and platform reliability.

What You'll Do

  • Design and implement new features across the full stack
  • Optimize application performance and scalability
  • Collaborate with product and design teams to build great user experiences
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
  • Participate in code reviews and technical discussions
  • Help establish best practices and technical standards

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience with TypeScript/JavaScript
  • Strong experience with React and Node.js
  • Experience with databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
  • Familiarity with real-time systems and API design
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Railway, or similar)
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and collaboration abilities

Nice to Have

  • Interest in sports and competitive gaming
  • Experience with Prisma ORM
  • Background in building consumer-facing products
  • Experience with real-time features (WebSockets, SSE)
  • Knowledge of testing frameworks and practices

Company Culture

At Parlay Streak, we're building a platform that combines sports, competition, and technology. We value:

  • Collaboration: We work closely together, sharing ideas and supporting each other's growth
  • Ownership: You'll have real impact on the product and the ability to shape its direction
  • Learning: We're always exploring new technologies and approaches to solve problems
  • Balance: We believe in sustainable work practices and supporting team members' well-being
  • Sports Passion: Many of us are sports fans, and that enthusiasm drives what we build

You'll have the opportunity to wear multiple hats, learn new skills, and see your work directly impact users. We're remote-first and value clear communication, autonomy, and results.

How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief note about why you'd be a good fit to careers@parlaystreak.com with "Senior Full-Stack Engineer" in the subject line.

We review all applications and will reach out if there's a potential fit.
