Responsible Gaming

Our Commitment

Parlay Streak is designed for entertainment and competition. We are committed to promoting responsible participation and providing resources for users who may need support.

Setting Limits

While our platform uses a streak-based scoring system rather than real money, we encourage users to:

  • Set time limits for platform usage
  • Take regular breaks from the service
  • Maintain a healthy balance with other activities
  • Recognize when participation becomes problematic

Warning Signs

Be aware of signs that may indicate problematic use:

  • Spending excessive time on the platform
  • Neglecting responsibilities or relationships
  • Feeling anxious or distressed when unable to access the service
  • Using the platform to escape problems or negative emotions

Account Management

If you need to take a break, you can:

  • Contact us to temporarily suspend your account
  • Delete your account if you wish to stop using the service
  • Adjust notification settings to reduce engagement

Resources

If you or someone you know needs help with problematic gaming behavior, consider these resources:

  • National Council on Problem Gambling
  • Gamblers Anonymous
  • Contact your local mental health services

Age Restrictions

Parlay Streak is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Contact

For concerns about responsible gaming, contact us at support@parlaystreak.com.
